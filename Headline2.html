Catching cruise and insulting people every second won't get you where
you want to be - Yul Edochie tells Nigerian youths

Actor Yul Edochie has dished out an advice to Nigerian youths over some of their activities online.
Edochie who stated that he has nothing against Nigerian youths, said insulting people every second won't get them where they should be.
The actor admitted that the government has not done well, but that there's so much youths can achieve if
they focus on self improvement.
He tweeted;
I have nothing against Nigerian youths. Just trying to wake them up. Catching cruise & insulting people every
second won't get you where you want to be. Yes the Govt hasn't done well but there's a lot you can achieve if you
focus on self improvement. Whatever you focus on grows.
